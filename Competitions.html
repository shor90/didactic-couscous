Competitions

Billiard is a sport of precision and patience, so that means intense brain activity when it comes to strategies and a good sense of physics

Every competition requires discipline and a great amount of power regarding the fast decision making

In preparation for our weekly competitions, we have designed 3 types of contests for different levels of players, so everyone has a chance to prove their skills

All the competitions details and sign-ups, will be available in the affichage board, or at the club's reception
