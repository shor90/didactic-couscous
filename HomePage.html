Welcome to Billiards World

In a world of stress, work, commitments, and other things that keeps you away from living your life at its potential, we invite you to a refreshing and engaging adventure in the world of billiards/pool. Here you can find many entertaining activities which mainly involves “putting balls in the holes”. If you are a competitive person, or just want to have a great time with friends, family, this is your getaway from your boring days. We have prepared for you, classes for learning the multiple branches of this sport.

For you and your friends/family, we have game bundles for extended time, and also including beverages and snacks. We have weekly competitions for our most passionate customers, and attractive prize for the winners. The Billiards World is a club for every kind of person, no matter the gender, age, or disability. We are encouraging more and more people to join this amazing sport.

Join us!
